# Mechanic taxonomy — the combination space

Generate a concept by picking **one base archetype** (or fusing two), **swapping or adding one or two
axes**, applying **exactly one twist**, and choosing **a theme**. Each axis maps to real plugins so
the idea stays buildable.

## The 9 axes

1. **Spatial frame** — open world · island · plot · box arena · one-block · grid of plots · earth map ·
   void · shrinking border · floating layers.
   (Plugins: BentoBox/SuperiorSkyblock2, PlotSquared, WorldGuard regions, Multiverse, a border plugin.)
2. **Core loop** — gather-craft-build · mine-sell-rankup · fight-loot-extract · claim-defend-raid ·
   quest-progress · collect-trade · survive-the-night · capture-objective.
3. **Progression** — skills (mcMMO / AuraSkills) · rankup + prestige · custom items + levels
   (MMOItems/MMOCore) · tech/unlock tree · seasonal reset · cosmetic unlocks.
   (Plugins: mcMMO, AuraSkills, LuckPerms tracks, MMOCore/MMOItems, BetonQuest.)
4. **Conflict** — co-op (none) · consensual PvP · open PvP · team war · raid-claims · arena rounds ·
   1v1 duels. (Plugins: WorldGuard pvp flags, a factions/claims plugin, CombatLogX.)
5. **Risk / stakes** — none · item loss on death · hearts (lifesteal) · ban on death (hardcore) ·
   extraction (lose carried loot on fail) · permadeath per season.
6. **Persistence** — permanent · seasonal reset · rolling map · ephemeral rounds.
7. **Social unit** — solo · party · faction · town · nation · global co-op.
   (Plugins: Towny, a factions plugin, parties in a core plugin.)
8. **The twist (the spice — exactly one)** — see the twist bank below.
9. **Theme / skin** — medieval · cyberpunk · post-apoc · mythology · pirate · space · liminal ·
   cottagecore · steampunk. (Plugins: ItemsAdder/Oraxen for custom items, a resource pack.)

## Twist bank (pick ONE, then ask "what plugin or Skript makes this true?")
- The map is **one block** everyone shares, and it changes phase as the server mines it.
- **Blocks decay** over time unless reinforced (forces upkeep).
- You can **steal another player's island/base** once you out-level them.
- The **economy is the only weapon** — no PvP, you bankrupt rivals out of regions.
- You **visibly age**; abilities shift by life stage, then you **reincarnate** with partial skills.
- **Water/lava rises** on a clock; race to extract loot before it floods.
- **Death makes you a ghost** who can still build but not fight, until revived.
- The **world shrinks** each day (border closes), funnelling players together.
- **Gravity/physics flips** at night.
- A **shared global goal** unlocks server-wide perks (co-op meta-progression).
- **Trust is mechanical** — you can betray a teammate for their loot, with a bounty cost.

## Fusion patterns that work
- **PvE grind that graduates into PvP** (OneBlock → raidable at phase N). Strong escalation.
- **Cozy base-building + a timed high-stakes window** (build by day, extract at the flood).
- **Identity/role progression + a reset** (aging → reincarnation prestige).
- **Solo island + a shared economy/war layer** (skyblock islands that can trade or raid).

Rule of thumb: the base gives players something familiar to grab in 60 seconds; the twist gives them
a reason to tell a friend. Map every axis to a real plugin, or mark the twist as Skript/custom.
