<h3>
  Features
</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Brew alcoholic Drinks, that actually can make you drunk</li>
  <li>Drunk people will stagger, the chat is influenced, effects and more</li>
  <li>Wake up at /home after a long night of drinking</li>
  <li>Lower quality brewed drinks have downsides like hangover</li>
  <li>Process of brewing with different steps like fermenting, distilling, aging</li>
  <li>Refine the quality, until the Drinks are perfect</li>
  <li>Master the brewing of even the most difficult Drinks</li>
  <li>Create your own Recipes including ingredients, properties and creation process</li>
  <li>Configurable influence on the chat</li>
</ul>

  <br/>
  <br/>
    Getting Started
  </h3>
  <br/>
  <br/>
  <p>
    After installing you may simply start brewing, by fermenting ingredients, distilling and aging the ingredients. Check the Wiki for detailled informations about the process of brewing.​
    <br/>
    <br/>
    To also take advantage of the more advanced features, you may want to have a look at the config, where you can create your own recipes and even alter the word replacements in chat when a player is drunk. By default there are some basic recipes, that also show what settings you can utilize. There are plenty more possibilities to create own recipes such as other Liquors, Special Drinks, etc. You may want to look around on the internet about how the Drinks are made, or create fantasy recipes with funny effects and ingredients.
    <br/>
    Most of the good Drinks should be kept secret or have just vague descriptions on how to brew them. That allows players to try around on specific ingredients, so that only certain brewers master the creation of a high quality drinks.​
    <br/>
    <br/>
    When it comes to drinking, it is nice to define Wakeup Points. These are places in the World where players "wake up" when they log off for a period of time while drunk. These places may be anything like high on a tree, inside a cave, on towers, on small islands or in astonishing buildings.
    <br/>
    For best results the places should be distributed over a big area, as the algorithm favors a place near the players current position.​
  </p>

<h4>
   BreweryX offers a variety of additions over the original verson of Brewery. This list is not exhaustive, but showcases some of our work on this plugin.
</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Customizable plugin prefix</li>
  <li>Full RGB support for 1.16+</li>
  <li>Multi-puke items</li>
  <li>Ability to specify custom ingredients by CustomModelData</li>
  <li>Support for BreweryX addons (Things like <a href="https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/breweryrecipes-breweryx-addon.114897" target="_blank">Recipes</a>)</li>
  <li>Patches like <a href="https://github.com/DieReicheErethons/Brewery/issues/541" target="_blank">these</a> </li>
  <li>Support for BreweryX in our <a href="https://discord.gg/3FkNaNDnta" target="_blank">Discord</a></li>
  <li><a href="https://github.com/PaperMC/Folia" target="_blank">Folia</a> support</li>
  <li>Constantly adding new things, fixing bugs, and giving life to Brewery!</li>
</ul>

<div align="center">
    <h3>
              Important Commands
                    <br/>
      Command ----------------- Description
  </h3>
    <ul>
    <li>/brew help ----------------- Shows the help page​</li>
    <li>/brew info ----------------- Information about your own drunkeness​</li>
    <li>/brew info <player> ----------------- Information about the drunkeness of <player>​​</li>
    <li>/brew create <recipe> ----------------- Create a Brew by Command​​​</li>
  </ul>

  <!-- Breaks -->
  <br/>
  <br/>
  <br/>
  
  <h3>
              Important Permissions
                    <br/>
      Permission ----------------- Description
  </h3>
  <ul>
    <li>brewery.user ----------------- Access to everything needed for brewing (default)​</li>
    <li>brewery.mod ----------------- Allow to maintain Wakeup Points and to login even if overdrunken​</li>
    <li>brewery.admin ----------------- Give access to every Command and most bypasses (Default: op)​</li>
  </ul>

  <br/>

  <h4>
    By Default, the Plugin is in english language and config. It is <b>advised to use the config and language</b> file for your language, as some parts of the plugin (like the Chat distortion) are language specific.
